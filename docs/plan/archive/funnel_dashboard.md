## 🌪️ Dashboard Philosophy: The "Pipeline X-Ray"

The Execution Funnel Dashboard answers one critical question: **"Where is my alpha dying?"**
Instead of just showing the final trades, it visualizes the *attrition rate* of signals. It transforms the 6-stage funnel (Universe → Ingestion → Alpha → Router → Risk Gate → SOR) into a visual flow, allowing you to instantly spot bottlenecks, over-filtering, or data decay.

---

### 🏆 Row 1: The Macro Funnel (The "Hero" View)

**Panel 1.1: Signal Attrition Sankey Diagram (The Most Important Panel)**

- **What it shows:** A flowing Sankey diagram showing the volume of symbols/signals at each stage over the last 24 hours.
  - *Flow 1:* Total Exchange Pairs (e.g., 500) → **Liquidity/Volatility Filter** → Dynamic Universe (e.g., 25)
  - *Flow 2:* Dynamic Universe (25) → **Data Freshness Gate** → Valid Snapshots (e.g., 24)
  - *Flow 3:* Valid Snapshots (24) → **Alpha Bridge Scoring** → High-Confidence Signals (e.g., 5)
  - *Flow 4:* High-Confidence Signals (5) → **Strategy Router** → Formatted Intents (e.g., 4)
  - *Flow 5:* Formatted Intents (4) → **Risk Gate** → Approved Orders (e.g., 2)
  - *Flow 6:* Approved Orders (2) → **SOR Execution** → Filled Trades (e.g., 2)
- **User Education:**
  - "This is your pipeline health. A healthy funnel narrows gradually. If it drops to zero at the Risk Gate or AI Analyst, you have a configuration problem, not a market problem."
  - "Hover over any 'drop-off' branch to see the exact rejection reason."

**Panel 1.2: Funnel Conversion Rate Gauges**

- **What it shows:** Percentage conversion rates between critical stages.
  - Data Freshness Rate: % of universe symbols with <500ms latency.
  - Alpha Hit Rate: % of universe symbols scoring above the entry threshold.
  - Risk Gate Pass Rate: % of router intents approved by the Risk Gate.
- **User Education:**
  - "If Risk Gate Pass Rate is < 20%, your risk parameters are too tight for current market volatility."

---

### 🔍 Row 2: Top of Funnel (Universe & Data Health)

**Panel 2.1: Dynamic Universe Composition (Pie/Bar Chart)**

- **What it shows:** The current active watchlist, categorized by why they were selected (e.g., "High Volatility", "High Volume", "Funding Divergence").
- **User Education:**
  - "Ensures the bot is actually looking at the right assets. If your target asset isn't here, the funnel stops before it begins."

**Panel 2.2: Data Freshness & Dropout Monitor (Time Series / Heatmap)**

- **What it shows:** Millisecond latency of WebSocket data per symbol, with red spikes indicating data staleness (>500ms).
- **User Education:**
  - "If a symbol constantly shows red here, the Alpha Bridge is scoring it with stale data. The system should auto-drop it from the universe."

---

### 🧠 Row 3: Middle of Funnel (Alpha & Routing)

**Panel 3.1: Alpha Score Distribution (Histogram)**

- **What it shows:** A histogram of all Z-scores generated by the Alpha Bridge in the last 24 hours.
- **User Education:**
  - "Should look like a normal distribution (bell curve) centered at 0. If it's heavily skewed, your feature normalization is broken."
  - "The vertical line shows your entry threshold. Everything to the right is a potential trade."

**Panel 3.2: AI Analyst Overlay Impact (Scatter Plot)**

- **What it shows:** X-axis: Strategy Router Score. Y-axis: AI Confidence Score. Color: Green (Agreed), Red (Direction Mismatch).
- **User Education:**
  - *Directly addresses your recent blocker.* "This shows exactly when and how strongly the AI disagrees with the router. If all red dots are clustered at high router scores, the AI is using a different timeframe or hallucinating."

---

### 🛡️ Row 4: Bottom of Funnel (Risk Gate & Execution)

**Panel 4.1: Risk Gate Rejection Reasons (Pareto Chart)**

- **What it shows:** A bar chart of *why* trades were blocked, sorted by frequency.
  - Bar 1: AI Direction Mismatch (e.g., 45%)
  - Bar 2: Slippage Tolerance Exceeded (e.g., 30%)
  - Bar 3: Max Concurrent Positions Reached (e.g., 15%)
  - Bar 4: Missing Atomic SL/TP (e.g., 10%)
- **User Education:**
  - "This is your debugging goldmine. If 'AI Direction Mismatch' is #1, you know exactly which lever to pull to unblock Shadow trades."

**Panel 4.2: SOR Execution Quality (Time Series)**

- **What it shows:**
  - Line 1: Intended Entry Price vs. Actual Fill Price (Slippage in bps).
  - Line 2: Time from "Risk Gate Approved" to "Exchange Acknowledged" (Latency in ms).
- **User Education:**
  - "Proves whether your infrastructure (VPN, API weight) is fast enough to capture the alpha the top of the funnel generated."

---

### ⚙️ Row 5: Funnel Controls & Debugging (The "Tuning" Row)

**Panel 5.1: Live Funnel Parameter Overrides (Text/Table Panel with Links)**

- **What it shows:** Current active thresholds, with links to adjust them (if your Grafana setup supports variable templating or external links).
  - Alpha Entry Threshold: `0.80`
  - Max Slippage: `0.50%`
  - AI Veto Confidence Threshold: `0.85` *(Highlighted as the key lever to pull)*
- **User Education:**
  - "Don't change code to test a theory. Adjust the AI Veto Threshold here to 0.90 and watch the Risk Gate Rejection bar chart shrink in real-time."

**Panel 5.2: Recent Funnel Drop-offs (Live Log Table)**

- **What it shows:** The last 20 signals that died in the funnel, with columns:
  - Timestamp | Symbol | Stage Failed | Reason | Router Score | AI Confidence
- **User Education:**
  - "Click on any row to expand the full JSON payload. This lets you see exactly what data the AI saw when it decided to veto the trade."

## 🚨 Alerting Matrix for Funnel Blockages

To prevent you from having to stare at this dashboard, configure these specific Grafana alerts:

| Alert Condition | Severity | Message Example | Action Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Data Freshness Dropout** > 3 symbols for > 2 mins | 🟡 Warning | "Data pipeline degrading. 4 symbols dropped due to >500ms latency." | Check Gluetun/VPN and WebSocket logs. |
| **Risk Gate Pass Rate** < 10% over 1 hour | 🔴 Critical | "Funnel blocked. 90% of signals rejected by Risk Gate/AI." | **Immediately** check Panel 4.1 for the dominant rejection reason. Adjust AI threshold or slippage limits. |
| **Alpha Score Skew** (Avg score > 0.9 or < -0.9 for >1hr) | 🟡 Warning | "Alpha Bridge normalization failure. Scores are not mean-reverting." | Check data ingestor for stale or corrupted feature inputs. |
| **SOR Latency** > 1000ms for 5 consecutive trades | 🔴 Critical | "Execution latency critical. Alpha is decaying before fills." | Check Bybit API rate limits and server CPU load. |

## 🎯 How to Use This Dashboard to Solve Your Current Blocker

You mentioned: *"AI consistently disagrees with strategy router direction. Shadow trades: 0."*

Here is your 3-step diagnostic workflow using this exact dashboard:

1. **Look at Panel 4.1 (Rejection Reasons):** Confirm that "AI Direction Mismatch" is indeed the top bar, consuming 100% of the rejections.
2. **Look at Panel 3.2 (AI Overlay Impact):** Check the Y-axis (AI Confidence). Are the red "mismatch" dots sitting at `0.55` confidence, or `0.95` confidence?
   - *If they are at 0.55:* The AI is just mildly uncertain. **Action:** Raise the AI Veto Threshold to `0.80`. The funnel will immediately open up, and Shadow trades will flow.
   - *If they are at 0.95:* The AI is *very* sure the router is wrong. **Action:** Click a recent drop-off in Panel 5.2. Read the AI's reasoning. Is it looking at a 4H chart while the router is looking at a 1m chart? If so, fix the AI's data payload timeframe.
3. **Look at Panel 1.1 (Sankey Diagram):** Watch the flow. Once you adjust the threshold, you should visually see the flow from "Strategy Router" → "Risk Gate" → "Filled Trades" light up with volume.
